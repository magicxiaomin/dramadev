## Guardrails to preserve

- Do not assume repository paths; verify with Git.
- Do not commit or push unless explicitly asked.
- Keep P0 scope free of backend/API route, payment, auth, database, Facebook/analytics, secret, and release-infrastructure changes.
- Prefer deterministic checks before subjective review.
- Treat automated labels and comments as advisory until a human reviews.
