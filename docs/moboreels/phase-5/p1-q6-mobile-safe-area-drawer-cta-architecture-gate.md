## P0 invariant preservation

Invariant, quoted exactly:

```txt
/variant-b/watch/[showId]?episode=1&source=facebook -> free episode chain -> first locked episode -> Unlock Drawer -> fake unlock/pass -> same episode with unlocked=1
```

No-regression constraints for any later Q6-related work:

- The ad landing route remains `/variant-b/watch/[showId]?episode=1&source=facebook`; Q6 cannot redirect users to Home, Search, Genre, or Show Detail.
- Free episodes remain watchable before login, recharge, PWA install prompt, Story Pass promotion, payment-like copy, real video infrastructure, or analytics/Facebook processing.
- The first locked episode remains determined by `episode > story.freeEpisodes && unlocked !== true`; Q6 cannot move or soften the first-lock boundary.
- The first locked episode opens the Unlock Drawer automatically; closing the drawer leaves the user on the same locked episode; tapping the locked playback area reopens the drawer.
- Primary CTA remains single-episode unlock (`Unlock EP X` or existing fake insufficient-balance wording inside the drawer); Story Pass remains secondary and cannot visually outrank primary on the first lock.
- Fake unlock and fake Story Pass must return to the same show and same episode with `unlocked=1`; no Home redirect, no episode loss, no required search/re-discovery.
- Locked state remains visually distinct from weak-network, offline, video-error, or generic-error states.
- `unlocked=1`, balance, cost, and pass outcome remain fake review/demo signals only and cannot become entitlement, wallet, account, purchase, or production state.
